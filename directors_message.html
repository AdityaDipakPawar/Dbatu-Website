Prof. Dr. Arvind W. Kiwelekar
Registrar
Head of Department

It is my pleasure to introduce the Department of Computer Engineering at the main campus of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere.

The Department is focused to impart quality technical education to the students so that they will be ready to serve software as well as other sectors of the industries. For better prospects in professional life, we aim to get Industry-sponsored internships and projects for our B. Tech and M. Tech students. As you all know that after graduation, you will become a software professional/developer in the IT industry. So you should strive to achieve good technical and non-technical skills and knowledge sets. The opportunities will knock on your door but you should be always ready to grab it. I believe that to become a successful person in life, it is necessary to set a goal and put sincere and constant efforts in the right direction to achieve it. Our Department will guide and help you to equip yourself with ethical values which help you to live a long professional life. To survive in this competitive and dynamic world, equip yourself with the latest technologies and trends. As a Computer Science and Engineering student, computer programming should be your bread and butter. Your every thinking should result in a small program that can be written in the latest programming language. Last but not least, we must not forget that while living professional life we should hold high moral and ethical values.

I welcome you all and wish bright and successful career ahead.
